Aphorism

Benford’s law of controversy

Passion is inversely proportional to the amount of real information available

本福德争议定理：激情与有价值的信息量成反比。

Betteridge’s law of headlines

Any headline which ends in a question mark can be answered by the word ‘no’.

Betteridgede的头条定理：任何以问号结尾的新闻头条都可以用一个子回答：不。

Brooks’law

Adding manpower to a late software project makes it later.

布鲁克法则：如果一个软件项目跳票了，添加人力只会让它跳票更严重。

Cunningham’s law

The best way to get the right answer on the internet is not to ask a question, it’s to post the wrong answer.

Cunningham法则：在网络上得到正确答案的最佳方式不是发布问题，而是发布错误答案。

Dilbert principle

The most ineffective workers are systematically moved to the place where they can do the least damage: management.

Dilbert原则：最低效的员工会被系统地安排到他们能造成伤害最小的岗位：管理。

Godwin’s law, an adage in Internet culture

As an online discussion grows longer, the probability of a comparison involving Nazis or Hitler approaches one

古德温定理（关于网络文化）：随着一项线上讨论的时间变长，关于纳粹或希特勒的类比出现的概率趋近于1。（于1990年提出）

Hofstadter’s law

It always takes longer than you expect, even when you take into account Hofstadter’s law

霍夫斯塔德定理：实际花费的时间永远比你预计的要长，即使你考虑了霍夫斯塔德定理。

Niven’s law

If the universe of discourse permits the possibility of time travel and of changing the past, then no time machine will be inbented that universe.

Niven定理：如果一个宇宙中穿越时间不能改变过去，那么在这个宇宙中就不会发明时间机器。

Stigler’s law

No scientific discovery is named after its original discoverer.

Stigler定理：没有一条科学理论是根据其原始发现者命名的。

Tobler’s first law of geography

Everything is related to everything else, but near things are more related than distant things.

托布勒地理学第一定律：所有事物之间都有联系，但距离近的比距离远的更相关。

Wiio’s law

Communication usually fails, except by accident.

Wiio定理：交流通常会失败，除非出现意外。

Wirth’s law

Software gets slower more quickly than hardware gets faster.

维尔特定律：软件变慢的速度永远比硬件变快要块。
